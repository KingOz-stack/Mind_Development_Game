Mind Development Game

Overview

The Mind Development Game is a web application designed to enhance focus and mindfulness. It offers interactive exercises such as breathing, gratitude journaling, and the "Moment" game. Users can also connect with verified psychologists for professional support.

Features

Exercises

  • Breathing Exercises: Guided timers to promote relaxation.
  • Gratitude Reflection: Journaling prompts for positivity.
  • The Moment Game: Focus-enhancing exercises targeting attention and mindfulness.

Sentiment Tracker

Analyzing user sentiments and sharing tips.

Connect with a Psychologist

  • Search and filter verified psychologists.
  • Real-time chat and appointment scheduling.

Gamification

  • Track streaks, time spent, and achievements.
